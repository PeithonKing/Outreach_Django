About Us

   The Outreach Program at NISER aims to communicate the importance and ideologies of Science with the community. This will help in positive contribution towards science education and technology, thereby enhancing the overall development of the society.

   As a part of this program, NISER, since it’s inception has been reaching out to students and teachers in nearby schools to motivate them in their scientific pursuit. The activities are coordinated and spread round the year in three categories:

  •   Educational Visits of Schools,
  •   NISER Open Day,
  •   Teacher-training Workshops under the Vigyan Pratibha Project.


Our vision

   To provides a single window for services such as online and offline education from school to undergraduate level, research programs, online portal for various learning units for schools, online teachers’ webinars, online and on-site teachers’ workshops, spiritual heritage programs for national and international audiences on current scenario, publication of books, etc. We started with NISER Educational trips where schools and colleges visit different departments at NISER. Then we moved on to Open day program in which NISER becomes open to all and people from various institutions/ schools can visit NISER for scientific exposure along with learning. Recently we have started teachers’ training workshop in collaboration with HBCSE, Mumbai and IOP, Bhubaneswar in the form of Vigyan Pratibha project.
